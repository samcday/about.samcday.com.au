Career

I've been here and there. Some people paid me to be in some of those places. Here's who:

  • Wotifgroup Pty Ltd

    Intermediate Software Developer
    September 2011 - Present

    Wotif has been a very special experience for me, primarily because it is the first large-scale company I've worked for - both in traffic/revenue and number of fellow employees.

    During my brief time at Wotif, I have already established myself as a very confident and proficient developer, able to tackle any task big or small. I have gained great rapport with my coworkers and my superiors alike. Together with the teams I have worked with, I have released several high projects on the main Wotif.com site successfully. These includes Wotif.com's foray into Holiday Rentals, Hotel Reviews, and many other enhancements (cosmetic and functionality) on the main site.

    The Wotif.com Reviews project in particular was an amazing experience for me, as I was instrumental in making its release a success. This success led to some very positive feedback to come from middle and upper management of Wotifgroup Pty Ltd.

    On top of the project successes I've had at Wotif, I've also chaired a developers forum and sat on the Social Committee.

    I'd like to think that in my time at Wotif I've helped to inspire and uplift those around me, just as I have been inspired by many of my peers in the Milton office.

    TL;DR
    • Worked on several high profile projects on main Wotif.com site.
    • Demonstrated impressive depth and breadth of technical knowledge.
    • Committed in many extra-curricular company duties.
    • Worked very closely and effectively with every discipline: Business Analysts, Project Management, Architecture, Operations staff, Senior & Lead Developers, fellow Developers.

  • Sauce Software Pty Ltd

    Web Developer
    2009 - Present

    At Sauce Software, I was responsible for initial estimation, analysis, design, implementation and deployment for many medium sized web applications for a range of clients. Many of the projects I worked on were time and budget sensitive, and I was able to deliver quality results on time and on budget in most cases. I was also tasked with maintaining several original IP systems Sauce had implemented.

    In additional to programming duties, I was also the system administrator for Sauce's production environment, where a large number of domains were being hosted. I was also responsible for selling and implementing the idea to move to a virtualized hosting environment, which cut hosting costs in half for Sauce, and provided a higher level of fault tolerance and isolation of our shared hosting clients.

    When it came time for me to move on to Wotif.com Pty Ltd, I was asked to remain involved with Sauce. I still assist with system administation duties, estimation and development work, and other consultation services.

    TL;DR
    • Involved in every phase of SDLC for many medium sized web applications
    • Delivery of projects on-time, and on-budget.
    • Linux system administration of several servers
    • Conceptualized and handled migration to virtualized hosting environment for increased reliability and reduced hosting costs.
